Your World
Your universe becomes a constellation.
Your worlds have been forced into fragments. Characters in one app. Lore in another. Timelines in a spreadsheet. Themes in a notebook. Rules in your head. Chapters scattered across files that don’t speak the same language.

Nothing connects. Nothing remembers. Nothing understands the shape of your universe except you.

Altalune changes that.
Your world becomes a constellation. Not a folder. Not a stack of documents. Not a maze of disconnected notes.

A living structure — where every panel knows every other panel. Where chapters link to lore. Lore links to timelines. Timelines link to rules. Rules link to themes. Themes link back to characters. Everything is connected. Everything is aware. Everything is part of the same universe.
Your world becomes coherent. When you update a character, the chapters that use them shift with you. When you change a rule, the lore that depends on it updates. When you adjust a timeline, the events ripple outward.

Your universe behaves like a universe — dynamic, relational, alive.
And your world becomes yours again. No subscriptions. No cloud lock‑in. No exporting. No syncing. No losing track of where anything lives.

Your world lives in one place. One structure. One constellation. One universe that grows with you instead of against you.
This is what Your World means. Your universe, unified. Your ideas, connected. Your creativity, finally given a place big enough to hold it.
