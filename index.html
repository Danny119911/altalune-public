What is Altalune?

Altalune is a portable creative OS designed for writers who think like engineers. It blends mythic‑scientific tone, narrative continuity, and AI‑assisted worldbuilding into a single, elegant toolset.

Whether you're building a seven‑book saga or a single universe with deep internal logic, Altalune helps you maintain clarity, structure, and creative sovereignty.
