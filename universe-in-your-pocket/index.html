A Universe in Your Pocket
Your creativity, sovereign and portable.
Your ideas have never truly belonged to you. Every app demands an account. Every platform demands a subscription. Every cloud service demands your data. Your worlds live on servers you don’t control, behind logins you don’t own, inside systems that can disappear the moment a company decides they’re done supporting you.

Your creativity has been held hostage by the internet.

Altalune ends that.
Your universe becomes portable. Not synced. Not streamed. Not stored “in the cloud.”

Yours.

A self‑contained world that lives entirely on your device — your stories, your lore, your timelines, your characters, your rules, your voice, your AI, your constellation.

Everything you create stays with you. Everywhere you go.
Your universe becomes private. No servers. No accounts. No analytics. No tracking. No silent uploads. No “improving our service” pipelines. No one watching. No one collecting. No one mining your imagination.

Your universe exists offline — unreachable, unmonitored, untouchable.
Your universe becomes sovereign. You can carry it on a portable hard drive. You can back it up anywhere you want. You can open it anywhere, anytime, without permission from anyone.

Your universe is not a service. It is a possession.
And Altalune leaves no footprint behind. It doesn’t install itself. It doesn’t scatter files across a system. It doesn’t write hidden folders, registry entries, caches, or logs. It doesn’t cling to the machines you borrow.

Wherever you go — library, school, work, hotel, café, a friend’s laptop — Altalune runs entirely from your drive, and when you unplug it, nothing remains.

Your universe arrives with you. And it leaves with you.
Your universe becomes eternal. No subscription to maintain. No server to shut down. No company to trust. No expiration date. No dependency on the internet’s mood.

Your universe lives as long as you want it to. And no one can take it away.
This is what A Universe in Your Pocket means. Your creativity, sovereign. Your worlds, portable. Your ideas, protected. Your universe — finally, truly — yours.
